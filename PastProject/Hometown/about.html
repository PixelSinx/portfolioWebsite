Frazee

Frazee is a town that is known for its history in turkey farming, and many of its residents have grown up doing something involving turkeys at one point or another. There was even a statute erected to symbolize this part of Frazee.

Frazee Wrestling

Frazee is also known for its high school wrestling team, and how well they have done through the years and its annual turkey days event that is held there every summer.
