Frazee Attractions

Frazee is home to a couple of attractions that you need to checkout when you are there.

Attractions

Worlds Largest Turkey

Frazee is home to the worlds largest turkey. The turkey was constructed to symbolize Frazee history of turkey farming, and how much it effected Frazee's residents many years ago. The statue that you can visit today was built in 1998 after the original burnt down.

Scrap Metal Moose

Frazee is also home to a moose made out of scrap metal on Main Street, next to a small goose pond. The statue was built in 1997 by Paul Epling
