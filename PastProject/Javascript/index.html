AMD and NVIDIA, The Next Generation.

( words)

AMD: Polaris

AMDS new GPU (Graphics Processing Unit) architecture, codenamed Polaris will be the first GPU released by AMD that is built on a 14nm process. This will allow AMD to double the amount of transistors from 2015s Fiji architecture, increasing the amount of transistors from 8.9 billion, up to 18 billion transistors.

NVIDIA: Pascal

NVIDIAS new Pascal architecture, aims to increase the power and efficiency of previous generations, by moving to a new 16nm architecture. Along with this new architecture NVIDIA will be unveiling its new NVLink Technology, which will allow the GPU and CPU to transfer data up to 5 times faster than PCIE 3.0.

Conclusion

Both AMD and NVIDIA are making huge strides towards more efficient and powerful graphics processors, while also developing new technologies that increase the productivity and continutity of all devices in your PC.
