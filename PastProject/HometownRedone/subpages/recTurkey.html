World's Largest Turkey

The big turkey statue that resides in the outskirts of the town symbolizes the turkey industry that used to be big part of Frazee. The new statue which you see when you visit today was constructed after the original one burned down, after a spark caught the paper mache body on fire while maintenance crews were using a blowtorch to do maintenance on the metal frame of the statue.

Location: Hwy 87, Frazee, MN

Directions: US Hwy 10 exit north onto Hwy 87 toward town. On the right, on a hilltop, in Lions Park, just before the bridge.
