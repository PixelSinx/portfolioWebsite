Android

This page is designed to show all of the projects I worked on in my Android class.

In my first real Android assignment (as the first actual assignment was just a blank screen that said hello). I created an app the displays the picture of a recipe, and then allows you to go and look up how to make that recipe (I don't actually know if this recipe is real or not).

In the second Android assignment, I created an app that calculates the total cost to go and see a concert. You could input how many tickets you wanted, and then select from a list of bands to see how much the total cost was.

In the third assignment, I created an app that can convert a persons weight to either kilograms, or pounds, based on whichever one they selected.

In the fourth assignment, I created an app that calculates the total cost of a bundle of pictures, based on how many, and what size pictures you wanted.

In the final assignment, I created a tour guide app. The top two buttons link you to their respective web pages, and the last three are pictures of those places.
